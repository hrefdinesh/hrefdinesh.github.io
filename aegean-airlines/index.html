Airline Overview and Services

Air Arabia is a currently operating Middle Eastern scheduled low-cost airline with a base at Sharjah International Airport, in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates.

This carrier's 2-char IATA designator is G9, and its 3-character ICAO designator is ABY. Established 21 years ago in 2003, the airline is one of the mature carriers.

Air Arabia offers a web check-in option for the convenience of its passengers. The most recent version of Air Arabia baggage policy (last updated 2024) is available on the airline's official website via the link in the menu on the left.

Air Arabia logo history

Air Arabia is one of the most recognizable and successful airline companies in the Middle East. Its logo has gone through various changes since its inception in 2003. Lets take a look at the history of the Air Arabia logo and how it has evolved over the years.

The original logo was created in 2003, when Air Arabia was first established. It featured a red falcon silhouette with a white text above it that read Air Arabia in English. This logo was simple and straightforward and served to distinguish the airline from its competitors.

In 2011, Air Arabia decided to give its logo a facelift. The new logo featured a blue circle with the same falcon silhouette from the original logo in the center. This time, the text above the logo read Air Arabia in both English and Arabic. This was the first time that the company had included Arabic in its logo, which was a nod to its regional identity and helped to set it apart from other airlines.

In 2020, Air Arabia unveiled its latest logo. This version featured a more modern and simplified design. The falcon silhouette was replaced by a blue circle with a white line in the middle, and the Air Arabia text was now written in a stylish font with the Arabic text below it. This logo is easily recognizable and gives the airline a contemporary and sophisticated look.

The Air Arabia logo has gone through various changes over the years, but one thing has remained the same: its iconic falcon silhouette. This symbol of flight and travel has become synonymous with the airline and is easily recognizable to customers all over the world.

Through various redesigns, the Air Arabia logo has come to represent the airlines commitment to innovation and customer service. It is a reminder of the airlines commitment to providing the best service to customers and a symbol of the companys success.
